About Us

Welcome to Travela

Welcome to our travel company STAY BAE TOURS AND TRAVELS, where my partner and I are dedicated to providing exceptional services to our guests. With a passion for exploration and a commitment to creating unforgettable experiences, we strive to tailor each journey to meet the unique needs of our clients. Our goal is to ensure that every traveller feels valued and enjoys seamless adventures, whether it's a relaxing getaway or an action-packed excursion. Let us help you discover the world with comfort and ease!

With over a decade’s worth of experience, our expertise extends beyond the ordinary, specializing in orchestrating seamless group tours and managing medical conferences (and MICE). As a team, we’ve fine-tuned the art of travel, ensuring that every trip is not just a commute but an experience worth cherishing (okay, that’s probably a little too much but worth looking forward to and remembering for sure) where your team members have fun talking to us while we plan their journeys.
